Overview

This project is a culmination of the knowledge we have gained throughout this semester, as well as the inclusion of design and data structure aspects from previous courses. Our goal was to explore and implement various technologies/frameworks to expand our understandingof desiging and building a fully fledged, operational application. We set out to design a web application that would allow users to favorite and track specific sports teams and players while simultaneously viewing aggregate historical statistic data.
